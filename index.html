Questions

Good things to know

How far in advance should I order? +
For everyday bouquets and arrangements, 2–3 days notice is ideal so we can source the freshest blooms. For event florals, we recommend reaching out at least 4–6 weeks ahead, though we'll always do our best for shorter timelines.
Do you deliver? +
Yes! We offer local delivery across the Chicago area. Delivery fees vary by distance and are shared when you place your order. Pickup from our studio is always free. For events, our team handles full on-site setup and breakdown.
Can I customize an arrangement? +
Absolutely. If you have a color palette, a favorite flower, or a specific vibe in mind, let us know and we'll design something just for you. We love custom work, and it's where we get to be most creative.
What if I don't know what I want? +
That's completely fine. Tell us the occasion, your budget, and any colors or feelings you're drawn to, and we'll put together something you'll love. Some of our favorite arrangements have started with "I have no idea, surprise me."
Do you do weddings? +
We do! We design bridal bouquets, ceremony florals, centerpieces, and full venue installations. We're part of the Tu Planners family, so if you need both wedding planning and florals, we can make that seamless. Reach out and we'll put together a custom proposal.
How do I care for fresh flowers? +
Trim the stems at an angle when you receive them, change the water every 2–3 days, and keep them out of direct sunlight and away from fruit (the ethylene speeds up wilting). Every order comes with a care card with specific tips for the blooms in your arrangement.
